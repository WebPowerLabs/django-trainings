The Order That Determines the Outcome

01.14.2014 in Design by Jami Amidon 3 Comments
Web Video Business

I wanted to take a moment to share a power moment of understanding that my most recent Let Go and Live Group experienced. It was a moment of understanding why we are spiraling out of control emotionally, when we are feeling like we just cant stop the spinning and don’t know what to do to get ourselves together.

Most, if not all of us, will default into behaviors that are only short term “feel good” fixes; shopping, eating, drinking, sleeping, drugs, sex, new relationships, the list is long…Those only serve to distract for a moment and then we are right back to where we were before if not worse.

Let me suggest a permanent solution to STOP this behavior. Pay attention to the order though, because it truly determines the outcome. Events-Thoughts-Feelings-Actions.
