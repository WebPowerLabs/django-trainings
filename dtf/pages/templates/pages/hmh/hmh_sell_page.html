Heal My Heart™

We all need someone to walk beside us...

Share the pain and the joy of every day living...

Help us not let the lows get too low...

And be there with each other to celebrate the highest achievements.

If you are experiencing painful feelings from the loss of a family member, your pet, a move, a divorce, the loss of a job, any Meaningful Emotional experience, then this workshop is for you.

Grief is a powerful emotion...

...and we are taught to react to the emotional feeling in our hearts by using our minds. Unfortunately, our friends and family don’t know how to help us. The support they offer does little to help us deal with the pain that lingers in our hearts.

Do you recognize these statements made by others in your time of need?

Time heals all wounds.

If you just take more time to be alone, you will feel better.

Don’t feel bad, she/he is in a better place.

You really need to get a new pet and you will be happy again.

Just keep yourself really busy and you won’t have time to think about it.

Why don’t you just get away for a couple of days to get your mind off of it?

Ouch! Those words are painful, confusing, and didn’t help or you would be feeling better.

The Solution...

The Heal My Heart™ Workshop

This is a seven week program intended to help you overcome the pain caused by memorable losses that have occurred in your life. Grief is different for everyone. It is personal. Your feelings are beyond comparison to anyone else.

In this workshop you will be:

  • Equipped with the tools you need to move beyond the pain and experience peace.
  • Free to take on the day and have hope for tomorrow.
  • Encouraged in the midst of pain and overwhelm, you are not alone on this journey.
  • Able to see the possibility for the joyful and balanced life you are created to live.
  • Invited to a safe space to work through your process.

When does this start and how much time do I need to spend on this work?

You can get started right now and self pace through the workshop. You have access to it for a whole year and has been designed to be accessible to you 24/7. The workshop listening time is anywhere from 1 to 1 1⁄2 hr long. The “ownwork” takes roughly 30-45 min a week to complete.

Do I have to be a techie to do this?

Not at all. You do have to be able to click your mouse, know how to watch a video and print or view a pdf file by simply clicking and printing. We have a frequently asked questions page on the site if you get stuck.

Is this therapy?

No. We are not therapists. This program uses self healing processes. If you are under the care of a therapist or other doctor for mental issues, or taking medications for mental health issues or depression, we suggest you consult with your caregiver before taking this course.

Is this work only if someone or my pet has died?

No, remember GREIF is described as Getting Really Intense Emotional Feelings we don’t know how to deal with from negative Meaningful Emotional events that happen in your lifetime. This pain is stored in our minds and hearts. It can come from divorce, moving, change in relationships, losing a job...there are so many losses that people suffer. When you process through this pain, a whole world of possibility, joy, peace, and freedom is open to you.
