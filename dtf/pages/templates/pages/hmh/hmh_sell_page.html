Heal My Heart™

We all need someone to walk beside us...

Share the pain and the joy of every day living...

Help us not let the lows get too low...

And be there with each other to celebrate the highest achievements.

If you are experiencing painful feelings from the loss of a family member, your pet, a move, a divorce, the loss of a job, any ME experience, then this workshop is for you.

Grief is a powerful emotion...

...and we are taught to react to the emotional feeling in our hearts by using our minds. Society unknowingly offers us false information as we look to deal with these feelings. Unfortunately the response our friends and family offer us do nothing to help us with the hurt that remains present in our hearts.

Can you identify with these unhelpful and maybe even hurtful comments made by others?

Time heals all wounds.

If you just take more time to be alone, you will feel better.

Don’t feel bad, she/he is in a better place.

You really need to get a new pet and you will be happy again.

Just keep yourself really busy and you won’t have time to think about it.

Why don’t you just get away for a couple of days to get your mind off of it?

Ouch! Those words are painful, confusing, and didn’t help or you would be feeling better.

The Solution...

The Heal My Heart™ Workshop

This is a seven week piloted program intended to help you overcome the pain caused by memorable losses that have occurred in your life. Grief is different for everyone. It is personal. Your feelings and thoughts are incomparable to those of anyone else with regard to the relationship that has been changed by the death of your loved one.

In this workshop you will be:

  • Equipped with the tools you need to move beyond the pain and experience peace.
  • Free to take on the day and have hope for tomorrow.
  • Encouraged in the midst of pain and overwhelm, you are not alone on this journey.
  • Able to see the possibility for the joyful and balanced life you are created to live.
  • Invited to a safe space to work through your process.

When we experience loss, the feelings from other accrued losses over a lifetime can be magnified. In seven weeks the Heal My Heart™ Workshop is designed to answer the question we have been subconsciously and consciously asking since childhood, “How do I deal with this pain?”

Let’s begin the H.E.A.L.I.N.G.

1

Healing begins by completing the past so that we may begin again.

In this session, we will:

  • Help you to see what is sitting in the foundation of your own life.
  • Look at our experiences with the past Meaninful Emotional experiences.
  • Learn how we become so vulnerable to this pain.

"Just this one tool you taught me about how to change my thoughts has changed my life!”

-Joan

2

Each loss in individual

We will cover how we have been dismissing our feelings and learn to process with guidance and proper tools.

"I am free again.”

-Sandra

3

Allowing our emotions to control us is not the answer

Conversations left unsaid build negative emotional energy inside of us. This negative energy has a terrible effect on our health, finances, work, and other relationships. Who we are BEing inside of this pressure isn’t the “same person” we know we really are.

In this session, you will:

  • Discover who you are BEing when difficult circumstances present themselves.
  • Begin to allow the person you were born to BE come alive again.
  • See yourself authentically.

"I am joyful, energetic, and know where I am going with my life... I know where I will never go again.”

-Shaye

4

Learning to live in the present moment

Do you have regrets from things said or done in the past? Are you worried about the uncertainty of the future? Our future is created one present moment at a time. This is a powerful session.

Learn to “be here now” and create a whole new perspective that will bring awareness to opportunities we would not otherwise be able to see.

“There was a physical and emotional lightening. I literally feel lighter and I am presenting myself as a much more authentic me.”

-Jen

5

Integrity with the truth about our feelings

Accepting the reality of our relationships, including the one with ourself in its entirety allows us to be authentic.

This session is key to you finding the solution to end the cycle to unresolved pain.

We will take a bird’s eye view of our own life and reveal the patterns and habits we carry with us as burdens along our journey.

“I am looking forward to having a new and different relationship with my dad.”

-Catie

6

No longer stuck

This session is all about learning how to unlock the shackles that keep you stuck so you can be released to fly in the direction of your destiny.

“I had no idea I had that much pain stored inside of me. It feels great to finally let go of it.”

-Cat

7

Go in the direction of your destiny and embrace the possibilities.

By this session you will have learned:

  • How to become self-aware of the events that happen in your life, and the stories you are making up about those events.
  • How to eliminate the negative stories that keep you stuck.
  • How to prevent these stories from having power over you again.

Joy, peace, freedom and even a life of fun are possible to the person who is willing to take the little steps toward exchanging what she/he has known for what she/he has yet to learn.

"My focus is much better!”

-Jenifer
